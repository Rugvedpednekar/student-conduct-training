Office Systems

This section explains the systems used throughout Student Conduct workflow and how a Graduate Assistant should use them accurately in daily work.

Module Focus Systems Training

Learn the tools first, because most daily GA tasks depend on Maxient, Outlook, 3rd Millennium, and shared office resources.

What are the systems you are going to use?

As a Graduate Assistant in Student Conduct, you will mainly use Maxient, Outlook, 3rd Millennium, and the shared office drive. Each system supports a different part of the office workflow.

1. Maxient

Maxient is the main system used in Student Conduct. It is the primary conduct case-management platform used to organize and manage case workflow.

What Maxient is used for

  • Reviewing assigned cases
  • Checking student records
  • Reading incident reports
  • Scheduling hearings
  • Sending hearing letters
  • Completing CRFs
  • Reviewing prior case history
  • Tracking sanctions and conduct workflow

This is the most important system because much of the hearing and case process happens there.

Maxient Access and Training Links

  • Login: http://cm.maxient.com/hartford
  • If you are logging in for the first time, set up your Authenticator.
  • Workflow 1: General Work Flow Video 1
  • Workflow 2: General Work Flow Video 2
Important Maxient Practice

Before updating a case, confirm you are working on the correct student and the correct record. Always cross-check details carefully and ask for guidance if a record looks unclear, duplicated, or incomplete.

Maxient Reminders

2. Outlook

Outlook is the official communication system used in Student Conduct.

What Outlook is used for

  • Communicating with students and others
  • Receiving Public Safety reports
  • Receiving weekly updates from GIPP Grad for completed meetings
  • Checking Dave’s schedule in Outlook Calendar

Outlook Link

https://outlook.office365.com/mail/

Outlook Reminder

Check Outlook regularly throughout the day. Read messages carefully before replying, and always use a professional tone in Student Conduct communication.

3. 3rd Millennium

3rd Millennium is used for sanction completion tracking. It helps the office verify whether a student completed assigned educational courses.

What 3rd Millennium is used for

  • Checking whether a student completed sanctions like educational courses
  • Verifying completion before updating records
  • Searching by student ID, email, or name when needed

3rd Millennium Link

  • https://sms.3rdmil.com/
  • This is the login page for 3rd Millennium.
  • Check the password book for the username and password.

3rd Millennium Walkthrough

Verification Reminder

Do not mark a sanction as complete unless it has been properly verified. If completion is not appearing clearly, double-check the search method and confirm the record carefully before updating anything.

4. Shared Space

On the assigned Student Conduct laptop, you can access the shared office drive. This shared space contains important office resources and reference documents.

  • Look for the conduct shared drive on the laptop.
  • Open the GA folder inside that shared space.
  • You will find important documents such as the minimum sanction guidelines and overdue sanctions lists.
