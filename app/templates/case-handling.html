This section walks through the case lifecycle from intake through closure. A Graduate Assistant should understand how to review reports, support hearings, keep documentation accurate, and follow through on post-hearing responsibilities without skipping workflow steps.

Review the incident report carefully and determine whether the matter should move into Student Conduct workflow.

Reminder

Do not assume a report is straightforward. Read carefully and verify context before moving anything forward.

Review the available information, determine what may be missing, and identify whether follow-up or clarification is needed.

When a case is assigned to you, coordinate the hearing clearly and make sure the student receives the necessary information.

Before the hearing, confirm that the case file, hearing notes, and logistics are ready and organized.

Keep Maxient accurate before and after the hearing. Records should be neutral, complete, and up to date.

After the hearing, complete the CRF, send the hearing resolution, and track any sanction or workflow items that follow.

A case should be closed only after required communication, documentation, and tracking steps are complete.
