Hearing Preparation

Use this section to prepare for Student Conduct hearings in a consistent, professional, and policy-aware way. The goal is to ensure each student receives fair notice, a well-organized process, and accurate case documentation.

Module Focus Pre-Hearing Readiness

Confirm case details early so the hearing can proceed on time and without avoidable issues.

Pre-Hearing Workflow

Complete the following checks before the student arrives. This sequence supports procedural fairness and keeps hearing operations organized.

Professional Standard

Be prepared, objective, and confidential at every stage. If a detail is unclear, pause and ask Dave or your supervisor before moving forward.

1) Review the Full Case File

  • Review the incident report and related documents in Maxient.
  • Confirm that all relevant supporting materials are attached and legible.
  • Identify missing details that may require clarification before the hearing.

2) Confirm Charges, History, and Process Details

  • Confirm each charge or issue listed in the case is accurate and aligned to the report.
  • Review prior student conduct history only when appropriate for the case process.
  • Verify hearing date/time against the student schedule when feasible.
  • Confirm that proper notice requirements were met before the hearing.

3) Verify Logistics Before Meeting Start

  • Confirm hearing location (or virtual details) and ensure the space is ready.
  • Prepare notes, question prompts, and key facts to guide the conversation.
  • Review minimum sanctioning guidelines reference material before the meeting.
  • Keep all records organized and confidential during and after the hearing.
Do Not Skip Verification

Missing notice, inaccurate charges, or incomplete documentation can delay hearings and create avoidable process concerns. Verify details first, then proceed.
