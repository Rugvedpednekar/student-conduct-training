Overdue Sanctions

Use this page to review overdue sanction records from Maxient, clean the spreadsheet for follow-up, and quickly identify missing contact/deadline details before outreach.

Workflow Snapshot Export → Upload → Download

Always keep the original Maxient export unchanged. This tool creates a separate cleaned workbook.

What overdue sanctions are and why they matter

Overdue sanctions are assigned outcomes that have passed their listed completion deadline and still need follow-up. Timely tracking supports student accountability, hold decisions, and consistent case closure.

Reminder

Review each row carefully before outreach. Some sanctions are time-based, some depend on outside completion confirmation, and some may require supervisor context.

Step-by-step: pull the report from Maxient

  1. Sign in to Maxient and open the reporting/export area used for sanctions tracking.
  2. Run the Overdue Sanctions report (or equivalent overdue sanctions export view).
  3. Confirm expected fields are present (File ID, names, SID, email, sanctions, deadline fields, hold status).
  4. Export the report as .xlsx and save locally.
  5. Upload that file below, process it, and download the cleaned workbook.

What fields to review first

  • Student identifier quality: Full Name, Student ID, and Email.
  • Assigned Sanctions text (long sanctions should stay readable and wrapped).
  • Next Deadline and Next Deadline Reason for follow-up timing.
  • Hold in Place and File ID for internal tracking/coordination.

Excel helper tool

The helper creates a new workbook with a standardized detail sheet and summary counts. Missing email and missing next-deadline cells are highlighted to support fast triage.

Important

This tool only accepts .xlsx files exported from Maxient and does not overwrite your original upload.

Quick Checklist
  • Export completed from Maxient today
  • File saved as .xlsx
  • Required headers present (or close match)
  • Review highlights after download
