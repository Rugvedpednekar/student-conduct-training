Overdue Sanctions Tool

What this section is for

Use this section to review overdue sanctions from Maxient, identify students who need follow-up, prepare outreach, and save the updated file for the GA shared drive.

Reminder

Review each row carefully before outreach. Some sanctions may already be in progress, some may need confirmation, and some may require supervisor review before communication is sent.

Step 1: Open Analytics in Maxient

  1. Open the Analytics section in Maxient.
  2. In Choose your report, enter 53.
  3. Set the report filters as follows:
    Date Range: current academic year (example: 2026–2027)
    Choose the date of reference: Reported Date
    Case Type: Student Conduct
    Limit results to: Student Conduct
  4. Click Run this report.
Image 1: Maxient Analytics report setup for Overdue Sanctions.

Step 2: Sort the report and export to Excel

  1. After the report loads and all cases appear, sort the report by Next Deadline.
  2. Click on Next Deadline so the most recent cases are easier to review.
  3. Then click Excel to download the file to your local computer.
Image 2: Sorting by Next Deadline and exporting to Excel.

Step 3: Upload the Excel below

  • Upload the Excel file downloaded from Maxient into the tool below.
  • Select the correct From and To dates for the overdue review period.
  • Process the file and review the cleaned preview carefully before downloading.

Step 4: Email students who need follow-up

After you get the updated Excel, email students who need outreach in Outlook using the approved format below.

Email Format 
Hi,

This is a reminder that you have one or more overdue sanctions with the Office of Student Conduct and Conflict Resolution.

To review the details of your assigned sanctions and instructions for completing them, please refer to the hearing resolution letter sent to your email. To locate these communications quickly, search for the term “Maxient” in your inbox.

Please note that unresolved sanctions may result in a hold being placed on your student account.

If you are unable to locate your letter, have difficulty completing your sanctions, believe this notification was sent in error, or have any other concerns, please don’t hesitate to contact us at stconduct@hartford.edu.

Step 5: Save the updated file

  • After outreach is complete, update the Excel as needed.
  • Save the final file in the GA Shared Drive under the Overdue Sanctions folder.
Important

Upload only a valid .xlsx file exported from Maxient. The original export stays unchanged.

Quick Checklist
  • Analytics report code 53 used in Maxient
  • Current academic year selected
  • Reported Date chosen as date reference
  • Student Conduct selected for Case Type and Limit results to
  • Sorted by Next Deadline before export
  • Excel downloaded and uploaded below
Before sending outreach

Review the cleaned output, confirm the date range, check hold-related information, and make sure the email list is accurate.

Final file reminder

After follow-up is complete, save the updated Excel in the GA shared drive for Overdue Sanctions so the team has the most current version.
